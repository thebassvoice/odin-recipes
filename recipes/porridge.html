Porridge

Description

It is basically a very thick porridge made from finely ground corn meal, called mealie meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Mealie Meal/ White Cornmeal
  • 1 Litre Boiling water

Steps

  1. Heat some water in a pot or kettle until it boils
  2. start adding mealie meal bit by bit, stirring vigorously
  3. The Porridge is ready to serve
